Introduction

DragonBones Design Contest is now open to for entries!

The contestants are invited to submit a character artworks with skeletal animation created by DragonBones.

The contest is an animation design competition supported by AwayEffect.com and open to all designers from around the world. We hope this contest will help to boost the collaboration among worldwide gaming designers to create world-class beautiful artworks.
