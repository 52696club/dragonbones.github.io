Free & Open Source (MIT License)

DrangonBones does not cost a dime. Download and use it right away — no strings attached. And because it is Open Source, you're always in control: step through the code and learn from its internals.

Based on Flash Pro, support rendering with Starling

DragonBones contains a Flash Pro based plug-in and a starling based library. With DragonBones, you can design your characters in Flash Pro, export it to texture atlas and use starling to render. It’s a perfect game development workflow with Flash DNA. Convert your vector based flash design to GPU based flash game now!

Platform Independent

As DragonBones is built on top of Adobe's Flash™ technology, it runs not only in the browsers, but also on all major mobile platforms out there, including iOS and Android.
